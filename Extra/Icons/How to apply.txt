Right-click the Rainmeter icon in the tray.
Find where it says

[TrayMeasure]
Measure=Calc
Formula=1
TrayMeter=Bitmap
TrayBitmap=Vista Rainbar\Extra\Icons\rtm.ico


Change the last filename to the icon that you want, like for example 

TrayBitmap=Vista Rainbar\Extra\Icons\beta2.ico

Save, and restart Rainmeter
